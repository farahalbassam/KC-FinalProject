P o h - P i a - T o d - ( T h a i - S p r i n g - R o l l s )

- Ingredients

  • 1 packet large spring roll wrappers
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 300 ml vegetable oil - for frying

    • filling

  • 200 g minced prawns
  • 300 g minced beef
  • 20 g glass noodles - soaked in hot water till soft, then drained
  • 4 dried shiitake mushrooms, soaked in warm water
  • 300 g cabbage
  • 100 g carrot
  • 10 g spring onion
  • 3 coriander roots (3-inches of roots and stalk)
  • 4 large cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • ¾ tsp white sugar
  • ½ tsp white pepper powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil

    • sauce

  • 1 large red chili
  • 1 sprig or 1 tbsp coriander
  • 1 small bird’s eye chili
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 60 ml white vinegar
  • 4 tbsp white sugar

- How To Cook

  • 1. Prepare the Vegetables

  • 2. Cook the filling

  • 3. Make the Rolls

  • 4. Deep-Fry till Golden

  • 5. Make the sauce

  • Plate and Serve!
