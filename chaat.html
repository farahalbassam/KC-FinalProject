S A M O S A - C H A A T

- Ingredients

  • YOGURT
  • TAMARIND CHUTNEY
  • POMEGRANATE
  • FROZEN SAMOSAS
  • GREEN CHUTNEY
  • SEV
  • CHANNA MASALA
  • ONION

- How To Cook

  • 1. Keep your samosas ready - use either fresh or frozen. If using frozen samosas, use an air fryer to heat them. I air-fried my frozen samosas at 370 F for 8 minutes, flipping them over once midway for even frying. The samosas come out perfectly crispy and I prefer this method to heating them in the oven.

  • 2. keep all the other ingredients ready. Get a large serving plate and crush up the samosas coarsely. Place them on the plate.

  • 3. Top off the samosas with spoonfuls of channa masala. You can add as little or as much as you like.

  • 4. spoon over the beaten yogurt, followed by the green chutney and the tamarind chutney.

  • 5. After adding the chutney, sprinkle over some chopped red onion followed by pomegranate seeds.

  • 6. Add a generous amount of fine sev, and to finish the dish, sprinkle some chaat masala and roasted cumin powder.

  • And that's it, your samosa chaat is now ready to be enjoyed!
