T a c o

- Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce

- How To Cook

  • 1. Spray a skillet with cooking spray then add the beef over medium heat.

  • 2. Brown the beef until cooked through, then drain and return to the skillet.

  • 3. Add the chili powder, cumin, oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, tomato sauce and soy sauce to the beef and stir to combine.

  • 4. Turn the heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes. Serve in taco shells and top with desired toppings.
