- Ingredients

  • chicken
  • vegetable (your choice)
  • oregano
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • pepper
  • onion
  • cayenne
  • salt

- How To Cook

  • 1. Place the quesadilla in a dry skillet over low to medium low heat.

  • 2. Cover with a lid – this heats the filling through and melts the cheese faster, before the tortilla gets too brown. If you don’t have a lid for your skillet, use anything that will cover it – a larger skillet, baking tray, anything. Cook until underside is golden and crispy, then remove the lid.

  • 3. Flip over the folded edge -this ensures you don’t lose any filling! Use your hand on the uncooked side to hold it together.

  • 4. Cook uncovered until crispy and cheese is molten – about 2 to 3 minutes.

  • Cut in half and serve!
